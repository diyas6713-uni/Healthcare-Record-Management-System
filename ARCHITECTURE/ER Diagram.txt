ENTITY – RELATIONSHIP DIAGRAM
Healthcare Record Management System
--------------------------------------------------

ENTITIES:
1. PATIENT
2. DOCTOR
3. BILL
4. HISTORY


RELATIONSHIPS (Directional Text Description):

1. PATIENT – DOCTOR
   Relationship: Treated By
   A Patient (P) is treated by a Doctor (D).
   A Doctor (D) can treat multiple Patients (P).
   Cardinality: Many Patients → One Doctor

2. PATIENT – BILL
   Relationship: Generates
   A Patient (P) generates one or more Bills (B).
   Each Bill (B) belongs to one Patient (P).
   Cardinality: One Patient → Many Bills

3. DOCTOR – BILL
   Relationship: Issued By
   A Bill (B) is issued by a Doctor (D).
   A Doctor (D) can issue multiple Bills (B).
   Cardinality: One Doctor → Many Bills

4. DOCTOR – HISTORY
   Relationship: Consulted For
   A Doctor (D) is consulted for medical History (H).
   Each History (H) is associated with one Doctor (D).
   Cardinality: One Doctor → Many Histories

5. HISTORY – BILL
   Relationship: Generates
   Medical History (H) generates Bills (B).
   Each Bill (B) is generated from History (H).
   Cardinality: One History → Many Bills


NOTATIONS USED:
P – Multiple Patients
D – Multiple Doctors
B – Multiple Bills
H – Multiple Histories


SUMMARY:
Patients are treated by doctors, their consultation histories are recorded,
histories generate billing information, and bills are issued by doctors for
patients. This ER model ensures structured data management and relational
integrity across the healthcare system.
